About Us

Akram Sbitany & Sons Co., founded in 1961 by Akram Issa Sbitany, has grown into Palestine’s leading retailer of home appliances and electronics, recognized for excellence and innovation. Our journey is driven by core values—Customer Centricity, Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, Excellence, and Community Commitment—that guide our mission to provide exceptional products and services. With exclusive partnerships with global brands like LG, Beko, Dyson, Philips, and Apple, we ensure access to premium, cutting-edge technology. Supported by a vast network of retail stores and service centers across Palestine, we deliver a seamless customer experience, from purchase to after-sales care. Our dedication extends beyond business, reflected in our 'WE CARE' Corporate Social Responsibility program. By partnering with SOS Children’s Village in Palestine, we actively contribute to improving the lives of orphaned and vulnerable children, providing them with love, support, and brighter futures. At Sbitany, we believe our role is not just to offer quality products but to serve as a positive force in the community, strengthening trust and fostering meaningful connections with every customer we serve. This commitment to innovation, excellence, and community shapes who we are and ensures we remain at the forefront of the industry.
